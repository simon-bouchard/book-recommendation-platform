# Known Limits

- At least **10 ratings** required to unlock behavioral recommendations.  
- Cold-start recommendations rely only on favorite subjects + Bayesian popularity.  
- Metadata gaps still exist; enrichment is planned but not complete.  
- Daily training is required for the latest recommendations to take effect.  
- Chatbot rate limits apply: logged-in users get 5 requests/min and 40/day; anonymous users get 3/min and 10/day.
